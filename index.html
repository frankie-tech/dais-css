Since sscaffold is inspired by so many other minimalist frameworks:

  • normalize.css
  • milligram.css
  • CSS-Tricks Box Sizing
  • Andy Bell: A Modern CSS Reset
  • minireset.css
  • Skeleton.css

I have had to pick and choose what I want and what I could do without. I don't think I'll be documenting that, as it was not extremely well documented originally where things came from.
